One day, the family went through a full night of hunger, as they did not have any food, which they offered even to their young children, and here the patient wife asked her husband to bring them any food, so that she could feed her young.

The husband went to his friends, one by one, but none of them agreed to lend him a part of the money, and return it to him later, so the man chose his command, and he began to plead with his Lord, to arrange his affairs for him.
